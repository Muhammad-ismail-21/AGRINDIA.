Agriculture in Savadatti Taluk

Savadatti is a prominent agricultural area in Karnataka known for its rich soil and favorable climate. Major crops include pulses, jowar, and sugarcane, making it a significant contributor to the region's agricultural output.

Some major crops

  1. Jowar
  2. Pulses
  3. Sugarcane
  4. Groundnuts
  5. Sesame

Some minor crops

  1. Onions
  2. Brinjal
  3. Tomatoes
  4. Coriander

Weather Challenges

Savadatti experiences irregular rainfall patterns, which can lead to both drought and flooding. Farmers often face challenges related to soil erosion and water management.
